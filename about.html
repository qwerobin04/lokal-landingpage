About Lokal Delivery Express

Lokal Delivery Express is a cargo and freight company that specializes in food delivery that promotes comfort and accessibility to the netizens of Daet Camarines Norte. The company offers a wide variety of partner companies and establishment!

Are you feeling hungry? Thirsty? Let Lokal satisfy your cravings and have it delivered at your doorstep with ease! Choose from the different partner companies that Lokal has to offer. Why worry about being hungry or thirsty? Lokal Delivery Express offers you a convenient, efficient, hassle-free food delivery with comfort and ease. Level up the experience at Lokal Delivery Express.

Vision

Lokal Delivery Express as the number 1 Cargo and freight Company in Daet Camarines Norte that shall bring the customers fast, affordable, reliable delivery service; that shall manifest comfort and accessibility to all clienteles.

Mission

To deliver delicious and remarkable food and beverage from different partner companies of Lokal to match the standards of Daet Camarines Norte, to consistently provide our customers with impeccable service by demonstrating warmth, graciousness, efficiency, knowledge, professionalism and integrity to our work.

To have every customer who visits the websites and through our doorstep be greeted or acknowledged as soon as possible, and thanked at least once for allowing us to serve, and to leave the impression of excellency and determination to serve with dignity. To provide all who work with us the tools to be their best in a friendly, co-operative, and rewarding environment. To keep our concept fresh, exciting and on the cutting edge of the hospitality industry. To be a giving member of the Lokal Delivery Express and its Partners Companies/restaurants and to improve the quality of service, accessibility and comfort throughout Daet Camarines Norte.
